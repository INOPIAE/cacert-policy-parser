1 Introduction

The Configuration-Control Specification (CCS [COD2]) mandates the creation and management of a list of all documents controlled for formal audit and policy purposes (CCS [COD2] Section 2.1). This list is located at svn.cacert.org/CAcert/Policies/ControlledDocumentList.html.

This list is managed by the Policy Officer according to the Change provisions of CCS [COD2] Section 2.2.

In this list, CAcert Official Document (or "COD") numbers are allocated, see 1st column below. The allocation of a COD number indicates the document is on POLICY track, and plays a part in the overall CCS framework.
